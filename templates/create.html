Instructions

What is a QR Code?

A qr code is a scanable image that maps a user to a specified link. This could be a website, pdf etc. It is best to keep the QR code in the same shape, but feel free to make it bigger or smaller to fit where you need.

What do I need to do?

The only thing you need to do is to make your menu available to the public. This can be done by having your menu on your website or in a shared drive like google drive or dropox.

My menu is on my website

Go to where the menu is located on the website and copy the Url (include the full url like https://www.bunsen.ie/menu). That's it, complete the form at the bottom of this page using that URl.

Menu is on a shared drive

A share drive could be google drive, dropbox etc. Go to the shared drive folder, right click to see an option to allow link sharing, this will allow customers to see the file. Copy this link.

QR codes work better when the link is smaller. We advise going to Short url, copy your drive link here and retrieve a new short url for it. Note include full link with https:// ex. https://shorturl.at/gpvAW. With this new URL proceed to from at the bottom of the page.

My menu is just on my laptop

If you just create your menus on your computer then print them off, don't worry, you will just need to put this menu in a shared drive. We reccommend to use google drive. It is free to have a personal account. Once you create an account, you can upload your menu then follow the step above.

Can a QR code link be changed?

No it cannot. Once you generate a QR code, whatever link you provided will always be what the QR code maps to. It is possible to get a dynamic QR code where you can change the link but that will cost you a subscription fee. However, there is a work around to changing/updating your menu without having to reprint your QR code.

How to update Menu and keep the same QR?

Stored on website: Simply update your menu on your website and the QR code will point to the new menu (same place) automatically.

Stored as a google drive file, On your computer, go to drive.google.com. Click on the file you want to replace. At the top right, click More More. Click Manage versions. Click Upload new version. Then you now have a new menu with same QR code!

What if I have multiple menu's?

If you have multiple menu's like drinks, food, lunch, dinner etc. You could link to a drive folder instead and a customer will be directed there and can see all the menus under that shared folder.

Can I get a second code?

Yes you can. If you make a mistake the first time, sure just repeat the process to get the correct link to your menu. If you need multiple codes, fire away.

What happens next?

Once you fill the form below, download your QR code. Then, you can add this to image to a doc where you have company info and print it off. Attch it to a window or you could laminate it and put it on tables for people to scan. The rest is up to you.
